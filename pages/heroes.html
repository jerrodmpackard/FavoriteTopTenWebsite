MARVEL HEROES


Spider-Man (Peter Parker)

The friendly neighborhood Spider-Man is known for his relatability and the enduring message that "with great power comes great responsibility." He is often depicted as a young, intelligent, and somewhat socially awkward individual who juggles his responsibilities as a student, freelance photographer, and superhero.




Iron Man

Tony Stark's most notable power is his intelligence and ability to design advanced technology. He created a powered suit of armor, the Iron Man suit, which grants him superhuman strength, flight, an array of advanced weapons, and a high-tech heads-up display system. The suit is a masterpiece of engineering and versatility.




Doctor Strange

The Sorcerer Supreme, Doctor Strange, is Marvel's master of mysticism and magical arts. Doctor Strange is a master of the mystic arts. He can cast spells, create magical shields, open dimensional portals, and manipulate reality. He possesses knowledge of countless mystical and magical forces. The Eye of Agamotto is one of his powerful artifacts.




Black Panther

The king of Wakanda, Black Panther, showcases not only the strength but also the intelligence and leadership of this African nation. As the Black Panther, T'Challa has enhanced strength, agility, and senses due to a special herb and ritual that grants him superhuman abilities. He is also a highly skilled hand-to-hand combatant and a genius inventor. Black Panther's suit is made of Vibranium, a rare and nearly indestructible metal.




Thor

Thor wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, which grants him superhuman strength, agility, and endurance. He can control the weather, fly, and summon lightning and storms. He's virtually indestructible and possesses immortality. As the Norse god of thunder, Thor is one of the most powerful characters in the Marvel Universe.




Black Widow

Black Widow is a highly skilled and versatile combatant with expertise in hand-to-hand combat, marksmanship, and espionage. She is known for her agility, acrobatics, and mastery of various weapons and gadgets. Although she doesn't possess superhuman powers, her rigorous training and experience make her a formidable fighter.




Hulk

The incredible Hulk is a manifestation of uncontrollable power and the struggle to keep it in check. The Hulk possesses superhuman strength, endurance, and durability. His strength increases as he becomes angrier, and he is virtually indestructible. He can leap great distances and heal rapidly. The Hulk's intelligence varies, often depicted as childlike when in his Hulk form.




Captain America

The symbol of patriotism, Captain America represents the ideals of justice, honor, and courage. Captain America is a super-soldier, thanks to the experimental Super-Soldier Serum. He possesses peak human strength, agility, and endurance. He is an expert martial artist and strategist. His iconic shield is made of vibranium and can be used both defensively and offensively.




Moon Knight

Moon Knight's alter ego is Marc Spector, a former Marine, CIA operative, and mercenary who becomes a vigilante after a near-death experience in Egypt. He's often described as Marvel's answer to Batman due to his dark and gritty persona. Moon Knight, however, is unique in that he may have superhuman abilities, but they are tied to the phases of the moon, making him stronger and more resilient during a full moon.




Captain Marvel

After being exposed to Kree technology and the explosion of a Psyche-Magnitron device, Carol Danvers gained a range of superhuman powers, including super strength, flight, energy projection, and enhanced durability. She's often referred to as one of the most powerful superheroes in the Marvel Universe.
