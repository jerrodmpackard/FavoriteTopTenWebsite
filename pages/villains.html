MARVEL VILLAINS


Dr Doom

Doctor Doom, whose real name is Victor Von Doom, is a prominent supervillain in the Marvel Universe. He is a brilliant scientist, inventor, and sorcerer. Doom is known for his mastery of technology and magic. He wears a suit of technologically advanced armor with a metal faceplate, which both protects him and enhances his physical abilities.




Red skull

The Red Skull is known for his grotesque appearance, often depicted with a red, skull-like face, the result of an experimental serum. He is a Nazi agent and was originally Adolf Hitler's top assassin during World War II. After the war, he continued to be a prominent figure in the world of supervillainy.




Ultron

Created by the brilliant scientist Hank Pym (Ant-Man), Ultron is an artificial intelligence with a highly advanced robotic body. However, he quickly turns against his creator and becomes a major antagonist. Ultron is known for his hatred of humanity and his quest to eradicate it. He believes that the only way to bring peace to the world is by replacing humanity with machines.

Venom

Venom is a compelling character in the Marvel Universe, born from the fusion of an alien symbiote with individuals like Eddie Brock. This eerie, black-clad figure, marked by a large white spider symbol on its chest and a menacing grin, possesses a range of superhuman abilities, including immense strength, agility, and the ability to create web-like constructs.




Rhino

The Rhino is a supervillain in the Marvel Universe known for his immense strength and durability. His real name is Aleksei Sytsevich. He is a thug from Russia who underwent an experimental procedure that bonded a super-strong polymer to his skin, giving him a nearly indestructible rhinoceros-like hide. As the Rhino, he possesses the ability to charge at opponents like a living battering ram.




Mandarin

The Mandarin is a fictional supervillain in the Marvel Universe, primarily associated with Iron Man. His true name is unknown, and he is a wealthy, brilliant scientist and martial artist from China. The Mandarin possesses ten alien rings, each with a unique power, which he wears on his fingers. These rings grant him a wide range of abilities, including energy projection, matter manipulation, and mind control.




Lizard

The Lizard is a fictional supervillain in the Marvel Universe, primarily associated with Spider-Man. His real name is Dr. Curt Connors, a brilliant biologist. In an attempt to regrow his amputated arm, Connors used an experimental serum derived from reptile DNA, which inadvertently transformed him into a giant, humanoid lizard with a savage and primal personality. As the Lizard, he possesses superhuman strength, agility, and a regenerative healing factor.




M.O.D.O.K

M.O.D.O.K., which stands for "Mental Organism Designed Only for Killing," is a fictional supervillain in the Marvel Universe. His real name is George Tarleton, a former technician at the organization Advanced Idea Mechanics (A.I.M.). Through an experimental process, his intellect was greatly enhanced, but his body became grotesquely deformed, with an oversized head and stunted limbs. M.O.D.O.K. possesses superhuman intelligence and psionic powers.




Thanos

Thanos, also known as the Mad Titan, is a formidable supervillain in the Marvel Universe. He is characterized by his purple skin, massive physique, and deep obsession with death and power. Thanos possesses superhuman strength, durability, and intelligence. He is a strategic genius and a skilled fighter.
